Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

Access to clean water and sanitation is essential for the health and well-being of individuals and communities. This goal aims to ensure that everyone has access to safe and affordable drinking water, adequate and equitable sanitation, and hygiene facilities.

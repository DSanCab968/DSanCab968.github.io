Goal 1 aims to end poverty in all its forms everywhere. It emphasizes the need for social protection systems, equal access to resources, and resilience against environmental disasters.

Key Targets:

  • Eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere.
  • Implement nationally appropriate social protection systems.
  • Ensure equal access to resources and services.

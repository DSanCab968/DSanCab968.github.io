Responsible Consumption and Production

Goal 12 aims to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns. It is about doing more and better with less. This means using our limited resources in a sustainable manner while reducing our ecological footprint.

Key Targets:

  • 12.1 Implement the 10-Year Framework of Programmes on Sustainable Consumption and Production.
  • 12.2 Sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources.
  • 12.3 Halve per capita global food waste at the retailer and consumer levels.
  • 12.4 Responsible management of chemicals and waste.
  • 12.5 Reduce waste generation significantly.

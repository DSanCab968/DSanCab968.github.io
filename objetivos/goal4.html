Goal 4: Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all

Quality education is a fundamental human right and the foundation for sustainable development. It is essential for the empowerment of individuals and communities, and it plays a crucial role in reducing poverty and inequality.

To achieve this goal, we must ensure that all girls and boys have access to quality early childhood development, care, and pre-primary education, and that all youth and a substantial proportion of adults achieve literacy and numeracy.
